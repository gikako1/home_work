Goal-Oriented-Academy


About us:

G.O.A (short for Goal-Oriented-Academy) is a programming academy where children of all age learn programming.

But there's much more than programming here. The CEO of G.O.A, Nika Keshelava is doing everything to bring his community together,
By planning events such as, Hackathon and Goa-land.

Hackathon


In Hackathon, groups of programming students compete with eachother for prizes and a good time. they compete in puzzles ,which the G.O.A mentors give.
Not only that, but students also compete in physical strength, for example:push ups, pull ups, armwrestling etc.

Goa-land


Goa-land is an event available for any G.O.A. student and their families. you can bring your friends and loved ones to Goa-land to spend time together,
on a nice field trip, for completely free.
Here, you can do whatever you want, and with whoever you want. you can meet other G.O.A. students here and get to know each other better.
did i mention physical competetions? just like hackathon, you can also compete here with others in: pull ups, push ups, arm wrestling, pulling the rope, and sports.
if you want to brighten your future, by doing something productive and fun at the same time, join G.O.A., the only academy you'll ever need.
